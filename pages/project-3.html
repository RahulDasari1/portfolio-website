E-Commerce Website Project


The project is aimed at creating a fully functional e-commerce website where users can browse products, add them to their cart, make purchases, and manage their accounts.

Features

1. User authentication: Users can sign up, log in, and manage their accounts.
2. Product browsing: Users can view products, search for specific items, and filter products by category.
3. Shopping cart: Users can add products to their cart, update quantities, and remove items.
3. Checkout process: Users can proceed to checkout, enter shipping and billing information, and complete purchases.
4. Order management: Users can view their order history and track the status of their orders.
5. Admin panel: Admin users have access to an admin panel where they can manage products, categories, orders, and user accounts.
